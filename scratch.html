Kuroko's Basketball (in Japanese: Kuroko no Basket) is a Japanese sport manga serie from December 2008 to September 2014. Later, it was adapted to anime television since 2012.

Kuroko's Basketball is one of my favorites animes. ❤️

Plot:

The Teiko Middle School basketball team dominated basketball teams within Japan, winning the middle school Nationals for three consecutive years. The star players of the team became known as the "Generation of Miracles". After graduating from middle school, the five star players went to different high schools with top basketball teams. However, there was a rumor that there was another player in the "Generation of Miracles": a phantom sixth man. This mysterious player is now a freshman at Seirin High, a new school with a powerful, if little-known, team. Now, Tetsuya Kuroko–the sixth member of the "Generation of Miracles", and Taiga Kagami – a naturally talented player who spent most of his youth in the US, aim to bring Seirin to the top of Japan by taking on Kuroko's former teammates one by one.

Meet the Characters

Kuroko Tetsuya

He is the main protagonist of the manga, Kuroko no Basuke. He was the Phantom Sixth Player of the Generation of Miracles at Teikō Junior High who specialises in misdirection and passes. He now plays as a regular at Seirin High with the goal of bringing the team and Taiga Kagami to the top of Japan.


Kagami Taiga

He is the deuteragonist of the Kuroko no Basuke series as well as the ace and power forward of Seirin High. He is Tetsuya Kuroko's basketball colleague and friend who is determined to defeat the Generation of Miracles and become the best in Japan. Kagami has exceptional basketball talents and has been called "The Miracle who did not become one of the Miracles".


Aomine Daiki

He was the ace player of the renowned Generation of Miracles and was the former partner/light of Tetsuya Kuroko in Teikō Junior High. He currently plays as the power forward and ace of Tōō Academy.


Kise Ryota

He was the Generation of Miracles' small forward who now plays at Kaijō High. He is well known for his copying abilities, as he can copy any technique he sees, often with more power than the original. He also works as a model.


Midorima Shintaro

He was the vice-captain and shooting guard of the Generation of Miracles who now plays for one of the Three Kings of Tokyo, Shūtoku High. Very confident in his abilities, he often acts high and mighty around others (mostly his teammates).


Murasakibara Atsushi

He was the Generation of Miracles' center who now plays for Yōsen High. He claims that the only reason he plays basketball, is because he's good at it, he doesn't care for the sport at all.


Akashi Seijuro

He is the point guard and captain of Rakuzan High. He was also the captain for Team Vorpal Swords and Teikō Junior High, commanding leadership over the Generation of Miracles.
